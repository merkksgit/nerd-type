22/06/25 Announcement
Global Leaderboards Are Now Live!

Major Update: Global Leaderboards are now live! Compete with typists from around the world and see where your skills rank. Your scores are now automatically submitted to a global database, creating real-time competitive rankings. View the top 20 performers worldwide, filter by game mode (Classic, Custom), and track total games played across the community. 22/06/25 Feature
Privacy First: New Privacy Toggle

Privacy First: New privacy toggle in Settings allows you to opt out of data collection entirely. When disabled, all your game data stays local on your device and nothing is sent to our servers. Your choice, your data, your privacy. Toggle it anytime with the new "Share game data for leaderboards" setting or use the /data command during gameplay. Privacy-conscious nerds, this one's for you!

14/05/25 Feature
Space Between Words Feature

New feature: We've added a more realistic typing experience with an optional space-after-word setting. Toggle this feature in the settings menu to practice typing spaces between words, just like in real-world typing. This helps build proper typing habits and gives more accurate WPM measurements. The choice is yours: traditional word-by-word mode or the complete typing experience with spaces. Which will you master first, nerd?

19/04/25 Feature
Quick Commands for Classic Mode

New feature: Quick Commands for Classic Mode! Now you can customize your typing experience without opening the terminal. Just type a slash followed by a command—like '/mode hard' to switch difficulty or '/setinitial 15' to adjust starting energy. Use '/help' to view all available commands or '/status' to check current settings. All changes apply instantly, allowing you to tailor your challenge on-the-fly. Unleash your efficiency, nerds!
