14/01/25 - Make your desktop match your typing skills with our custom wallpapers! Available now in our GitHub repo. Deck out your desktop with some proper nerd aesthetics!

11/01/25 - New feature: Real-time character feedback! Ever typed something and wished you could see exactly where you went wrong? Well, now you can! We've added character-by-character highlighting that shows you exactly which keys you're nailing and which ones need some love. Orange for perfect hits, red for those rebellious misclicks, and gray for the keys waiting their turn. It's like having a tiny typing coach right in your browser! Debug your typing and optimize your input, nerds!

09/01/25 - New feature: Accuracy tracking! Ever wondered how precise your typing really is? Now you can find out! We've added accuracy tracking to both Classic and ZenMode, showing you exactly how many keystrokes hit their mark. Every character counts - no pressure though! Watch your accuracy percentage climb as you perfect those keystrokes. Keep it clean and precise, nerds!

From traditional "tonttu" (elves) to the sweet aroma of "pipari" (gingerbread) and the warmth of "glögi" (mulled wine), these new additions will bring the cozy Nordic Christmas feeling to your typing practice. Get ready to jingle all the way, nerds! Merry Xmas!

Now you can see your progress in both intense hacking sessions and peaceful typing flows. Time to find your inner zen, nerds!
