How Words Per Minute (WPM) is Calculated

In NerdType, your typing speed is measured in Words Per Minute (WPM). Here's how the WPM is calculated:

  1. The game tracks the time elapsed from the moment you start typing until the game ends. This time is converted from milliseconds to minutes.
  2. The game counts the total number of characters you typed correctly, excluding any incorrect words.
  3. The average word length is calculated by summing the lengths of all the words in the game's word list and dividing by the total number of words.
  4. The number of correctly typed words is calculated by dividing the total number of correctly typed characters by the average word length.
  5. Finally, the WPM is calculated by dividing the number of correctly typed words by the time elapsed in minutes.

For example, if you typed 200 characters correctly in 1 minute, and the average word length is 5 characters, your WPM would be:

(200 characters / 5 characters per word) / 1 minute = 40 WPM

Keep in mind that the WPM calculation only considers correctly typed words and does not account for any errors or incorrect words you may have typed.
