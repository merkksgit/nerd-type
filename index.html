22/06/25 Feature
Privacy First: New Privacy Toggle

Privacy First: New privacy toggle in Settings allows you to opt out of data collection entirely. When disabled, all your game data stays local on your device and nothing is sent to our servers. Your choice, your data, your privacy. Toggle it anytime with the new "Share game data for leaderboards" setting or use the /data command during gameplay. Privacy-conscious nerds, this one's for you!

UI Update: We've streamlined the word set selection! The word set selector has been moved from the game area into the settings panel for better organization. Access all word sets (English, Finnish, Swedish, Programming, Nightmare) through the settings panel or with the /lang quick command.

02/06/25 Feature
Keypress Sounds Added

New feature: We've added satisfying keypress sounds to enhance your typing experience. Each keystroke now produces a subtle audio feedback that helps you maintain rhythm and flow during intense typing sessions. The feature is fully customizable - toggle it on/off in the settings panel or use the quick command /sound during gameplay. Whether you prefer the zen silence or the tactile satisfaction of audio feedback, the choice is yours, nerds!

14/05/25 Feature
Space Between Words Feature

New feature: We've added a more realistic typing experience with an optional space-after-word setting. Toggle this feature in the settings menu to practice typing spaces between words, just like in real-world typing. This helps build proper typing habits and gives more accurate WPM measurements. The choice is yours: traditional word-by-word mode or the complete typing experience with spaces. Which will you master first, nerd?
