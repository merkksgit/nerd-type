22/06/25 - Major Update: Global Leaderboards are now live! Compete with typists from around the world and see where your skills rank. Your scores are now automatically submitted to a global database, creating real-time competitive rankings. View the top 20 performers worldwide, filter by game mode (Classic, Custom, Zen), and track total games played across the community. Visit the new Global Scoreboard to see how you measure up against the fastest fingers on the planet. All data is stored securely with Firebase technology, and usernames are displayed publicly on the leaderboards. Ready to claim your spot in typing history, nerds?

03/06/25 - UI Update: We've streamlined the word set selection! The word set selector has been moved from the game area into the settings panel for better organization. Access all word sets (English, Finnish, Swedish, Programming, Nightmare) through the settings panel or with the /lang quick command.

02/06/25 - New feature: We've added satisfying keypress sounds to enhance your typing experience. Each keystroke now produces a subtle audio feedback that helps you maintain rhythm and flow during intense typing sessions. The feature is fully customizable - toggle it on/off in the settings panel or use the quick command /sound during gameplay. Whether you prefer the zen silence or the tactile satisfaction of audio feedback, the choice is yours, nerds!

14/05/25 - New feature: We've added a more realistic typing experience with an optional space-after-word setting. Toggle this feature in the settings menu to practice typing spaces between words, just like in real-world typing. This helps build proper typing habits and gives more accurate WPM measurements. The choice is yours: traditional word-by-word mode or the complete typing experience with spaces. Which will you master first, nerd?

29/04/25 - UI Update: Zen Mode is now integrated directly into the game settings! No more switching between pages - simply toggle Zen Mode on/off in the settings panel for a seamless transition between pressure-based and relaxed typing experiences. This streamlined approach keeps all your gameplay options in one convenient location. Enjoy the simplified experience, nerds!

23/04/25 - Are you just a script kiddie, or have you earned admin privileges? New achievements just dropped – prove your skills and unlock them all, nerds!
